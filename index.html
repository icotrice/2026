About The Conference

Join leaders and innovators from around the world

ICoTriCE 2026 is the 3rd International Conference on Trends in Chemical Engineering, dedicated to exploring cutting-edge innovations in green process engineering, sustainable energy solutions, and advanced materials. This hybrid conference brings together leading researchers, industry professionals, and academics from around the world to share knowledge, network, and collaborate on solutions for a sustainable future.
